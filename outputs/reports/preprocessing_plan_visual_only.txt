PREPROCESSING PLAN VISUAL ONLY
======================================================================
scope: image-level visual synthetic-media verification
scope_refinement: TrueScan was originally scoped as a multimodal (visual + audio) deepfake
detection system. During Phase 3 the active project team changed from
three members to two. To preserve implementation depth, reproducibility,
and evaluation rigor for the remaining two members, we refined the scope
to image-level visual deepfake detection only. Audio detection (ASVspoof,
WaveFake, AASIST, LFCC features) and video-level temporal aggregation
are deferred to future work.
future_work:
  - audio deepfake detection (ASVspoof / WaveFake / AASIST)
  - video-level temporal aggregation across frames
  - multimodal fusion of audio and visual signals
dataset_plan:
  training_dataset: FaceForensics++ c23 (curated 1000-video subset)
  subset_composition: 200 real + 200 each of Deepfakes / Face2Face / FaceSwap / NeuralTextures
  external_test: Celeb-DF v2 (zero-shot, never used for tuning)
  split_design: identity-level 70/15/15; manipulated derivatives travel with their source identity
face_detection_and_crop:
  tool: MTCNN (facenet-pytorch) on selected DEVICE, with center-crop fallback
  min_face_size: 80
  confidence_threshold: 0.9
  margin: 30% around the bounding box
  output_size: 224x224
  fallback: center-crop + resize to 224x224 when no face is detected
  eda_motivation: EDA showed mostly clean frames; MTCNN locks classification onto the face region.
frame_sampling:
  frames_per_train_video: 16
  frames_per_eval_video: 8
  sampling_strategy: uniformly spaced indices over the full clip
normalization:
  mean:
    - 0.485
    - 0.456
    - 0.406
  std:
    - 0.229
    - 0.224
    - 0.225
  note: ImageNet normalization to match pretrained backbones
training_augmentation:
  horizontal_flip: p=0.50
  jpeg_compression: q sampled from {30, 40, 50, 60, 75, 90}, p=0.40
  gaussian_noise: sigma in [5, 25] / 255, p=0.25
  downscale_then_up: random factor 0.5..0.85, p=0.25
  color_jitter: brightness=0.2, contrast=0.2, saturation=0.1, p=0.30
  random_erasing: p=0.25
  mixup_cutmix: one of MixUp(alpha=0.2) or CutMix(alpha=1.0), p=0.50
  eda_motivation: JPEG/Laplacian analysis showed sharpness drops sharply below q=75; matching the augmentation distribution to in-the-wild compression expected at deployment.
frequency_features_aux:
  description: log1p(|DCT|) of the grayscale 224x224 face crop, used as a secondary input branch
  rationale: Section 7b shows distinct radial-spectrum signatures across manipulations; an auxiliary frequency branch lets the main model exploit them.
class_imbalance_handling:
  sampling: WeightedRandomSampler at the image level (inverse class frequency)
  loss: Focal Loss (gamma=2.0) blended with class-weighted BCE; label smoothing eps=0.05
leakage_prevention: splits are stratified at parent-identity level; an assertion in section 5 verifies zero overlap.
